Mission

Our mission is to deliver high quality golf software right to the palm of your hand. Starting humbly by our founder Conner Tucker in his dorm room, we set out to revolutionize the golf tech space with our wonderous new innovations.

Course Evaluator

Our patent pending course evaluator reshapes how golfers think about course difficulty. Each course is assigned a rating 1-100 based on factors such as average score, price, user review, and shape of holes. This gives the msot accurate reading possible on how difficult or out of the average range a course is.

Shot Recorder

Using the app on your phone, you are able to record each shot you take in a given round of golf. All you have to do is simply enable GPS and enter in what club you're hitting with, and where the shot landed. This gets recorded into the app for you to view whenever you please.

AI-Assistant

Try out our new AI-assistant caddie during your next round! With thousands of customizable options ot personalize your caddies personality and voice, the possibilities are endless. Your caddie will be able to comment during the round just like the real thing.

Club-Suggestions

After using the shot recorder, the app is able to tell you what club would be most effective at any given yardage. There are a feew key factors that are used to evaluate which club would be best suited. First your average club distance, other golfers club usage at that specific hole, and wind conditions.

More To Come
